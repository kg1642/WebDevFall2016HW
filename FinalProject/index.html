ACTIVITIES

Mountain Biking
Many mountain bike routes lay claim to the title of the World’s Greatest, but only on our mountain biking tour in Nepal will you be surrounded by five-mile-high peaks.

Mountain Climbing
Put your busy life on hold for a few weeks to go peak climbing in Nepal and the myriad of majestic Himalayas.

Paragliding
A world of nature and freedom is waiting for you. Paragliding always an unforgettable experience, for your own pleasure or as a gift.

Rafting
If you are looking for the best way to travel from Kathmandu to Pokhara – adventure capital of Nepal, then the Trisuli is certainly the best choice.

DESTINATIONS

Janakpur
Stroll through the ancient Mithila city to experience the Terai culture and visit the Ram Janaki Temple in the center of the city that draws allusions to the famous Hindu epic Ramayana.

Chitwan
Chitwan has forests, marshland and rippling grassland, and is home to sizeable wildlife populations. It’s little wonder this place is so popular amoung tourists.

Kathmandu
For many, stepping off a plane into Kathmandu is a pupil-dilating experience, a riot of sights, sounds and smells that can quickly lead to sensory overload.

Pokhara
Pokhara ticks all the right boxes, with spectacular scenery, adventure activities, and accommodation and food choices galore.
