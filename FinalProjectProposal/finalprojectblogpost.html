Final Project: Visit Nepal

I continued working on my Midterm project[Visit Nepal] for my Final project. I added more pages and more features. Pages and features that I will added described below:

  • I used boostrap so that the pages are more responsive. I also changed the how the nav bar works. Now, when you click on a certain destinations or activity it you scroll you to the div of that destination or activity and it reduces the brightnes of the image, increase its size and gives a brief intro about that destination or activity. It can be done from other pages as well. The same effect will take place if you hover over the images.

  • I added a Know Your Nepal Birthday Page. It also allows you to share the result in either Twitter or Facebook. I used twitter and facebook api, boostrap-date-picker for the maps and implemented the logic the change from English to Nepali date by coding in javascript

  • I also added the most popular tweets in the country in real time. I used the Twitter widget for it.

Problems I Faced and How I solved them

I also wanted to enlarge the image for just 5 seconds so that the user know which image is the one corresponds to that option they clicked. I could decrease the image to normal after 5 seconds but the effect of hover would not work.

  • I could not change how FB and Twitter share button look. This is because both FB and Twitter wont update by js until next week. I tried changing the div size but it still did not work. We would have to wait till next week to have a perfectly sized buttons.

    • I struggled a lot trying to override some of the boostrap nav classes. Although, not a good practise, I used !important multiple times and even included css under style tag in html documents.
